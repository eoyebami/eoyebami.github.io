### Overview

* AWS `Snow Family` consists of rugged physical edge computing and data transport devices that allow you to securely migrate massive volumes of data in and out of the cloud
    - bypassing bandwidth constraints and run compute workloads in remote or disconnected environments
    - allows you to transfer large sets of data much faster than going through the internet
        * they send you a device, you copy your data over, they receive the device, copy the data over to something like s3, and then they wupe the device to protect your data
* Members of the `snow family` include
    - `snowcone`: smallest device built for extreme environments, aerospace, or fleet data collection
        * offers up to 8TB of storage
        * have compute capability
            - great to have compute power in extreme enviroments
            - ruggized to handle these rough environments
        * extremely secure:
            - encryption capabilities with keys managed through `kms`
            - device supports a trusted platform module to ensure hardware-based root of trust
    - `snowball edge`" highly ruggedized device designed for data migration and local edge computing
        * offers up to 80TB of stage and high performance vcpus and gpus to run local `ec2 instances`
        * have compute capability
            - great to have compute power in extreme enviroments
            - ruggized to handle these rough environments
        * extremely secure:
            - encryption capabilities with keys managed through `kms`
            - device supports a trusted platform module to ensure hardware-based root of trust
        * NOTE: no longer available to new customers
    - `snowmobile`" exabyte scale data transfer service housed in a 45foot shipping container pulle by a semi truck
        - designed to migrate up to 100PB of data at once
